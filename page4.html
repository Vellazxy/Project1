This poster only uses simplified grids with black and white colors. I am really attracted to this minimalism style. Most of the situation, the simpler the shapes are, the harder it can be made into a good-looking design. The designer behind this poster is called Joseph Muller. The most amazing part of this poster is the circles. They seem to be put randomly on the poster, but there is actually a principle applied on them. The biggest circle is only shown a quarter of it and all the circles are connected in 45 degrees. The small details actually help form this great work.

This design of calendar attracts my attention immediately after I saw it. The shapes combined with numbers brings a feeling of digital computing art. The use of gradient on white background is so pretty that creates illusion of dream. Most of them use organic shapes for the cover and create a similar feeling of watercolor.

This is graphic designed book. The designer mainly uses primary color to compose the whole book cover. The most interesting part of this book I think is the cut-out part of the cover. The curve shape really adds its specialty to the cover. The colors of orange and blue are in contrast and bring visual impact to the audience.
